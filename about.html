🌸 Our Story

Born from a simple truth

Momsies was founded with the intention of simplifying and reducing the stress of preparing for motherhood. We recognized that many mothers struggle to find all the necessary items in one place.

We're four Jamaican friends who wanted every mother to feel supported, seen, and celebrated. Our mission is to give you time back, offer curated quality, and wrap you in care — from bump to baby & beyond.

💖 affordable • convenient • customizable

We deliver island-wide with love & reliability.
