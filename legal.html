🍪 Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: To enable core functionality like shopping cart and checkout
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your login status and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our website (via Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Cookies: To show you relevant offers and promotions

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until you delete them or they expire
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by Momsies directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by our partners (payment processors, analytics)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality, including your ability to make purchases.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics (for website analytics)
  • Payment processors (for secure transactions)
  • Social media platforms (for sharing content)

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, email us at hello@momsiesja.com.
